DEVELOPMENT OF A WEB PLATFORM WITH E-COMMERCE, APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING, AND INTEGRATED CHAT ASSISTANT FOR INNERMAN INDUSTRIAL TRADING'S PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

February 2, 2024

Many businesses and companies nowadays already have a digital identity. On the other hand, Innerman Industrial Trading currently lacks a comprehensive digital platform to effectively showcase and manage its extensive portfolio of products and services, resulting in significant challenges for both the company and its customers. Without an integrated e-commerce system, customers face difficulties in accessing detailed product information, making secure online purchases, and receiving timely support, which negatively impacts sales and customer satisfaction. The absence of an automated appointment scheduling system further complicates the process of booking and managing appointments, leading to inefficiencies and potential customer dissatisfaction. Additionally, the lack of an integrated chat assistant hinders real-time customer interaction, making it difficult to resolve inquiries promptly and provide personalized recommendations.
